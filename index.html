Stoker and Daisy, named after Bram Stoker author of Dracula. Daisy after the character of the same name from the Great Gatsby.

About them:

Daisy and Stoker were born March 3, 2019. Their mother was placed in a kill shelter, where she was rescued after having four kittens. They were put up for adoption by Pets Bring Joy, where they were named Rocky and Buttercup. In June of the same year they were adopted and brought to their forever home.

Daisy and Stoker are very playful cats, but also have a thirst for trouble. From getting into string and bins not for them, to dragging toys up the stairs to play at 3 am. However, they are also best friends who call to each other if one is in trouble or will search for the other if they are lost. Their days are spent with many naps and always playtime.
